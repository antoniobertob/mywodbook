Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2025

MyWodbook does not collect or store personal data on external servers. All user data is stored locally on the user's device.

Information stored locally:

  • Name (for profile display)
  • Date of birth
  • Gender

This information is used to generate personalized workouts and display stats. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

Data ownership: You own your data. If you delete the app, your data is removed from the device. If iCloud sync is enabled, data may also be stored in your iCloud account according to Apple’s terms.
